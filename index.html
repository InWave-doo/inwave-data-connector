Data Connector for Shopify

Automated analytics pipeline that extracts your Shopify store data and delivers clean, ready-to-use dashboards — revenue trends, order metrics, and store performance, updated daily.

Read-only access

The app only reads your store data (orders, products, customers). It never modifies, creates, or deletes anything in your Shopify store.

Daily automated sync

Data is pulled from your store once per day and transformed into business-ready analytics — daily, monthly, and yearly revenue breakdowns, customer counts, and order trends.

Multi-store support

Connect one or many stores. Each store's data is kept separate and tagged, so you can view them individually or compare across stores on a single dashboard.

Your data stays yours

Data is stored securely in your own infrastructure. Nothing is shared with third parties. See our privacy policy for details.

How it works

Once installed, the app connects to the Shopify Admin API using secure OAuth authentication. It reads your orders, products, and customer records, processes them through a data pipeline that cleans and validates the data, and outputs business-ready analytics tables.

These tables feed directly into your dashboard (Metabase, Power BI, or any BI tool), giving you up-to-date insights into revenue, order volume, and store performance — without manual exports or spreadsheets.
